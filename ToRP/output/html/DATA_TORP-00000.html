DATASET OVERVIEW

Description(s)

Abstract

Photographs of time-series plots of solar radio waves (1, 2, 3.75, and 9.4 GHz) observed at the Nagoya University Aerospace Research Institute (Toyokawa City) from 1958 to 1978 has been stored in microfiche form.

Resource Type

Dataset, Timeseries plots in solar radio wave
