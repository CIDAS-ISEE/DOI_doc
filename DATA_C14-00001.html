Description(s)

Abstract

Information of wood specimens for the California, Finlnad, and Switzerland series: (1) Pinus longaeva specimen 1971#059 from the Methuselah Walk site in the White Mountains of California, U.S. (37.3794°N, 118.1654°W, 3094 a.s.l.), (2) Pinus sylvestris specimen from Finnish Lapland (69.26°N, 27.40°E, 199 a.s.l.), and (3) Larix decidua Mill. specimen from the forefield of the Unteraar glacier, Switzerland (46°34’ N, 8°15’ E, 1930 m a.s.l.). The 14C concentrations in the California, Finnish, and Switzerland specimens were measured at ETH Zürich and Yamagata University (YU-AMS), at YU-AMS, and at ETH Zürich, respectively.
