DATASET OVERVIEW

Description(s)

Abstract

All-sky imager data taken by an optical mesosphere thermosphere imager (OMTI) with various filters at Darwin, Australia, in order to investigate the dynamics of the upper atmosphere through nocturnal airglow emissions. The image data consist of OI (557.7 nm), OI (630.0 nm), OH-bands, OI (777.4 nm), and background (572.5 nm) emissions.
