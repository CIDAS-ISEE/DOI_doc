DATASET OVERVIEW

Description(s)

Abstract

This database contains segmentation maps of solar Soft X-ray images (Hinode/XRT) , temperature image of solar corona (X-ray) and time series of X-ray irradiance, temperature and photospheric magnetic field (SDO/HMI) of coronal features for the Solar Cycle 24. In addition it is provided the time series data (E)UV irradiance(SDO/AIA, PROBA2/SWAP) of coronal features.

Resource Type

Dataset, XRT segmentation and temperature maps & time series of the coronal feature
